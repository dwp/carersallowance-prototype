You will need

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number, and your partner's if you have one
  • the date of birth and address of the person you’re caring for
  • details of any employment you've had in the last 6 months
  • details of any self-employed income you've had
  • your bank or building society details so you can be paid the money

The application will reset if you don’t do anything for more than 90 minutes - you’ll have to restart.
