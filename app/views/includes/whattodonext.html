What to do next

Gather together your evidence and any other information you need.

You'll have a 'to-do' on your Universal Credit account to arrange an interview to confirm your identity.

When you do that mention you also need an interview to prove you can apply for benefits in the UK. This may happen at the same time as your identity interview or on a different day.

Bring the evidence listed above to your interview.

Save and return to Universal Credit

You'll be able to see this information again from your Universal Credit 'to-do' at any time.
